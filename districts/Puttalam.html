Kalpitiya is a town near the coast located in Puttalam district. Kalpitiya beach is a marine life sanctuary There are so many reasons why people visit this place, some of them are: surfing, flying kites, sun bathing, watch whales and dolphines etc

Wilpattu National Park

Wilpattu is one of the oldest national parks in Sri Lanka. Wilpattu is another wild life sanctuary in the country. There are many lakes present in this park with relatvie to other national parks in the country. The park is also home to a vast vareity of flaura and fauna.
