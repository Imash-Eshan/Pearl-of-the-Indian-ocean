Muthiyangana Raja Maha Viharaya

Muthiyangana Viharaya is situated in Badulla. The specialty of this temple is that its history goes back to the time of Buddha. It is said that during the time of a visit to Sri Lanka by the buddha, his relics were granted and got enshrined in a Stupa known as the Muthiyangana Stupa. The temple of Muthiyangana was built around this stupa.

Dunhinda Falls

The Dunhinda falls has been recognized as one of the most elegants waterfalls in Sri Lanka. This waterfall has got its name to due the prescence of smoky dew drops. Due to its shape resembles that of a bridal veil, it is also called as Bridal fall. It is 64 in height. This fall is created by the Badullu oya.
