Ruwanweliseya

Ruwanweliseya a.k.a 'Mahathupa' has either 2 quarts or one Dona of the Buddha's relics making it the largest collection of the buddha's relics anywhere in the world. It was built by King Dutugemunu. Ruwanweliseya has been ranked among the tallest ancient monuments in the world. It is a masterpiece of architecture and a striking memorial to the artistic and engineering engunuity of the ancient Sri Lankans. It is a wonderful place to explore.

Abhayagiri Dagoba

Abhayagiri Vihara was one of the major monastery sites of Buddhism. It is that was located in Anuradhapura. It has been claimed to be as one of the most sacred Buddhist pilgrimage cities in the nation. Towards the North of the city surrounded by great walls and containing elaborate bathing ponds, carved balustrades and moonstones, stood "Abhayagiri", one of 17 such religious units in Anuradhapura and the largest among its other Viharas.
