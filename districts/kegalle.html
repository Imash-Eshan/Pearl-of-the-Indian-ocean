Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage

The name 'Pinnawala elephant Orphanage' is just as famous as the word 'Sri Lankan elephants'. This place is a sanctuary, orphange and a breeding ground for elephants around the village of Pinnawala. At present this place is home to 95 elephants making this place the largest captivated elephant container.

Asupini Ella

Asupini Ella is a beautiful waterfall located in Kegalle. This waterfall is a part of Maha oya, a reputed river in Sri Lanka. There are many stories about this waterfall making it even more tempting for visitors to visit this place.
