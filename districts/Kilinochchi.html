Chundikkulam National Park

Chundikkulam National Park is national park located 12 km from Kilinocchi. Chundikkulam Lagoon which is being surrounded by sea grass and mangroove swamps has composed together to form this place. This park is home to a diverse variety of bird species including but not limited to Northern pintail, oriental ibis, painted stork, ruff, shoveler, terek sandpiper and wood sandpiper.

Kilinocchi War memorial

Kilinocchi war memorial is a historical monument in Sri Lanka located in Kilinocchi district. This place was originally a cemetery for the deceased LTTE cades. At present this place is a commemoration for the Sri Lankan military forces due to the sacrifices made by them during the war.
