Henarathgoda Botanical Garden

There are 6 six botanical gardens in Sri Lanka out of which Henarathgoda Botanical Garden is one of them. This garden was created in 1825 by governer Edward Barnes when he used to garden around this area which got expanded later on to be the infamous Henarathgoda Botanical Garden. This garden is located 10 m above sea level and composed of an area of apporoximately 44 acres.

Asgiriya Raja Maha Vihara

Asgiriya Raja Maha Viharaya is a cave temple which is quite ancient. It has been built on top a rock. There are many paintings drawn on this temple which resembles thoses drawn during 89-77 BC. All these together with its complex structure has made this temple an important archeological site in the country.
