Victoria Park

The Victoria park is a park located in Nuwara Eliya. This park was once used to be a research center. This park's name has been placed in respect to Queen Victoria's Jubilee. The park is also a home to a number of rare birds including endemic birds.

Mountain Pedro a.k.a Piduruthalagala is the highest mountain in Sri Lanka rising upto 2524 m high. Due to the position of its higher level, the summit of this mountain is used for communcation actvities in the country.

Horton Plains National Park

The Horton Plains National Park is the park with the highest altitude among other national parks in Sri Lanka. Hortan Plains is the starting point for 3 of the 4 major rivers in Sri Lanka; Mahaweli, Kelani and Walawe. The park is rich in bio diversity.
