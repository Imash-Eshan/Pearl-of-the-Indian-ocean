Nallur Kandaswamy Kovil

The Nallur Kandaswamy Kovil constructed in 948 A.D. is a hindu temple located in Nallur Jaffna. This place is believed to be overlooked by a deity known as Lord Murugan. Many of the Annual Hindu festivals are celebrated in this place which makes this place an ideal spot to learn about the Hindu rituals and customs. Nallur Kovil is an important archeological and political site in the country.

Jaffna fort

Jaffna Fort was built during 1618 by the Portuguese under the leadership of Phillippe De Olivera. At the time of its creation , Jaffna Fort had been the one and only large military fort in the country. At present this place is a significant monument in Sri Lanka making it a highly popular travelling site in the country.
