Old Colombo Dutch Hospital

The Old Dutch Hospital is the oldest building around the area of Colombo fort. It dates back to the time of the Dutch rule in Sri Lanka. During an LTTE attack in 1996, this place suffered an extreme damage. But after that, it has been risen from the ashes and thrived to transform into a shopping and dining precinct simultaneously maintaining its historic architecture.

Traditional Puppet Art Museum

The puppet art is a part of many countries all over the world and it goes the same for Sri Lanka as well. Here in the Traditional Puppet Art Museum arts such as healing rituals, devil dances, and Kolam & Nadagam can been seen. The puppet artists here are very talented and are creative enough to showcast their artistic skills to the point that this meuseum has become a famous travel place in Colombo. An excellent place to get to know about both the present and past cultures of Sri Lanka.

The Dehiwala National Zoo

A place with a massive collection of exotic and indegineous animals while being one of the oldest zoos in Asia is the Dehiwala national zoo. The zoo currently contains but not limited to 89 species of Fish, 72 species of Mammals, 31 species of Reptiles,65 species of Birds , 03 families of Amphibians and 30 species of Butterflies etc. With the free liviing birds taken into count, it has been estimated that there are around 2500-3000 of animals living in this zoo.

Mount Lavinia

Mount Lavinia is known for its 'Golden miles' of beaches. Mount Lavinia is a special place because it is one of the most liberating areas in Sri Lanka. Mount Lavinia acts as the host to Sri Lanka's annual gay pride Rainbow kite Festival. The local area name 'Galkissa' came into occurance with the 2nd Sri Lankan governer Sir thomas Maitland due to the fact that he constructed his residence in here.

Gangaramaya Temple

Located on the Beira lake Gangaramaya is one of the most important temples in the country due to it being composed of modern architecture(a mix of Sri Lankan, Thai, Indian, and Chinese architecture) and the prevelance of cultural essence. Gangaramaya has been a home to infamous scholars such as Pandit Batuwantudawe, Ratmalana Sri Dharmarama Thero, Waskaduwa Sri Subhuti Thero and Weligama Sri Sumangala Thero.

Viharamahadevi Park

Viharamahadevi park is a public park located in Colombo. It was the British during their colonial rule. This is the largest and the oldest park in Colombo. The park was initially called Victoria Park but it was later changed to Viharamahadevi devi park. Orignally there was a statues of Queen Victoria which was later removed and replaced with a statue of the Buddha. A must visit eye catching and a relaxing place
