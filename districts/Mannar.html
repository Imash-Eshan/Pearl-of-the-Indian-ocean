Thanthirimale is an ancient village in Sri Lanka which has gotten famous due to the prescence of the temple Thanthirimale raja maha Viharaya. This temple was supposed to have been built during the 3rd Century BC.

Shrine Of Our Lady Of Madhu

The Shrine of our lady Madhu a.k.a Our Lady Of The Rosary of Madhu is a Catholic shrine with a history of over 400 years. This shrine is considered as the most sacred Christian shrine in Sri Lanka.
