Biso Pokuna is a square shaped pond used by a princess made entirely out of stone. The water in has pond has flowed into it from the hills surrounding it. At present Biso Pokuna is an archeological heritage of the country.

Menik ganga

The name 'Menik ganga' translate into 'the river of gems' due the fact that so many precious gems are hidden in the mud of this river. The Menik river is infamous due to it been the 13 th longest river in Sri Lanka. It runs through 2 provinces and is 114km long.

Katharagama

Kataragama is a sacred town of Buddhist, Hindu and indigenous people in Sri Lanka. There are lots of religious sites in Kataragama; Kirivehera Stupa, Kataragama dewalaya, Temple of Syncretism etc. Buddhists and hindus from all over the world visit this place to pay their homage to their respectives religions and philosophies.
