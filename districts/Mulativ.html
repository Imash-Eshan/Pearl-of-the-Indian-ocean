Mullaitivu

The War Museum (+Monument Of Victory)

The war Museum is a museum which holds primarily war items during the times of the civil war. This museum obtained its reputation due to the prescence of a special monument in its close vicinty known as the Monument of Victory.

The LTTEs Secret Submarines

During the times of the civil war, the LTTE commsioned a number of Submarines. Most of those Submarines were held at Mullaitivu. Now that the war is over the submarines still left have been used to display them to make the place a famous tourist place in the country.
