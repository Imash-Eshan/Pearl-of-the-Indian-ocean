Matara

Star Fort

The Star fort also known as Redoute Van Eck is a fort which is situated in Matara. This fort was constructed by the Dutch 1765. This fort was destroyed several times but has been finally restored to be a museum which gets visited by people all over the world.

As its name suggests, the Old Dutch Trade Centre aslo known as the Nupe Market was built by the Dutch in 1775. The trade ceenter had been abandoned for several decades until it was officially restored by the department of Archeology in 1980.

The Polhena beach is a place where you can enjoy the beach in a calm and relaxed manner due it being a place without crowds of tourists. Even the water of this beach is calmer when compared with other beaches. The infamous river Nilwala finds its way to the other via this beach.

Crow Island

In the close vicinty of the Polhena beach at the spot where the river Nilwala finds itself into the ocean, lies the crow island. The crow island has its own eco system containing but not limited to mangroove plantations and fresh water lakes etc which has made this into an elegant travelling place.
