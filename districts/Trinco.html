Koneswaram Temple

The Koneswaram Temple Trincomalee is known by several names; Thirukonamalai Konesar Temple, Dakshina-Then Kailasam and The Temple of the Thousand Pillars. Koneswaram temple is a highly devoted Hindu religious place. This temple is considered as the most sophisticated temple of during its time.

Fort Fredrick

Fort Fredrick a.k.a Trincomalee Fort is a fort which was built by the Portuguese during the year 1624. The fort was built on top of a rock called Swami rock. The fort was once destroyed during the war but was later restored by the Dutch during their reign. Today the Fort Fredrick is famous travelling place in Trincomalee district.

Velgam Vehera

Velgam vehera is a temple located in Trincomalee district. This place is known to Hindus as Natanar Kovil making this place an important religious site for both Buddhists and Hindus. This temple was built during the era of King Devanampiyatissa. This temple was destroyed a long ago but its ruins were found during 1929 and this places has been declared as a archeological site eversince.

Pigeon Island National Park

There are 2 marine national parks in Sri Lanka out which 1 is the Pigeon island National Park. The park is located 1 km away from the Nilaveli beach accounting to a total area of 471 hectares. The pigeon island consists of 2 parts namely; large pigeon island and small pigeon island. The park houses a vast variety of flaura and fauna.
